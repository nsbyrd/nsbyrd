Campus Climate (DEI) Survey

One of two undergraduates chosen to represent the student body at faculty DEI committee meetings to conceptualize, implement, and analyze a campus-wide survey assessing diversity, equity & inclusion.

The Body Talk

Co-founder & president of a new club on campus, The Body Talk (TBT). TBT aims to foster awareness of systematic issues related to body size and work toward building body positivity. We are an inclusive size diversity group for all gender identities.
